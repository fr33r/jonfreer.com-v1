Near the end of high school, I began to feel the pressure from my friends to attend The Ohio State University (OSU). Most of those who grow up in the Columbus area attend OSU.

However, I had gained interest in Miami University, which is located in the small town of Oxford, Ohio. I had played several hockey tournaments at Miami over the years and I had always liked the parts of campus that I was exposed to.

When it came to making a decision, I had both the papers signed for OSU and Miami. Last minute, I chose to take a another visit to Miami to validate what I truly wanted. There was no question after this. I wanted to be a Redhawk.

This was one the best decisions of my life. Every year I went back to school loving the places and the people I was surrounded by even more. I even loved the education more and more as the years went on.

In the four years I spent there, I made lifelong friendships, joined a great fraternity, met my beautiful girlfriend, received the best education I could ask for, and had a whole lot of fun in between.
