Shortly after I knew was going to play for my high school, I was given the nomination for trying out for Team Columbus from my high school coach.

Team Columbus is a team that aims to gather the best high school talent to play teams outside of Columbus. The team has a season that plays during the summer before the normal high school season starts.

Due to the large number of players in the 1992 and 1993 birth years, it was decided that there would be two, completely equivalent Team Columbus teams.

I was informed after earning a spot on the team that I was ranked number one goaltender that tried out for Team Columbus by one of the scouts that selected players for the two teams.

This was a great experience for me. It helped me transition from the AAA hockey atmosphere and got me accustomed to the highest talent I would face in the upcoming season. I also made friends with many of the players of other teams which made me feel like I was a welcome member of the high school hockey community.
